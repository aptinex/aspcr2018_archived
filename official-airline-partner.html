As the official airline partner for the ASPCR-SLCD 2018 Conference, Sri Lankan Airlines is honored to invite you to indulge in our traditional Sri Lankan warmth and ultimate luxury with 15% discount on Economy Class and 10% discount on Business Class, as you journey to Colombo from any Sri Lankan Airlines on-line destination.

Online Destinations

Indian Sub-Continent: Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Cochin, Bangalore, Trichy, Trivandrum, Madurai, Hyderabad, Visakhapatnam, Coimbatore, Pakistan, Maldives, Seychelles, Bangladesh

Far East: Singapore, Beijing, Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Shanghai, Canton, Tokyo, Indonesia, Melbourne

Middle East: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, Dammam, Jeddah, Doha, Kuwait, Muscat, Bahrain

Europe: London

Please note that to avail of these discounts, ticketing will have to be done directly at our UL offices and not through agents and proof of registration will be needed (a letter confirming participation by the local event-organizing body will suffice).

(All related taxes must be paid for separately and are not discountable)
