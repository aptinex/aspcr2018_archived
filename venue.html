‘The Kingsbury’ is primarily a five star business hotel which is poised along the ocean which makes it more a Semi Transient Resort. ‘The Kingsbury’ is located in Sri Lanka's commercial capital Colombo and is in close proximity to local city attractions and entertainment venues. The refurbished property was reopened in December 2012, as a 5 Star luxury property consisting of 207 Deluxe Rooms, 21 suites, a Spa and Gym facility and Swimming Pool. The guest rooms have been furnished with Italian branded Frette linen and signature Bvlgari toiletries.

The hotel offers the regal splendor of 3 ballrooms – ‘The Balmoral’, ‘The Victorian’ and ‘The Winchester’ with capacities ranging from 100 – 500 guests. It also consists of 3 meeting rooms. Three elegantly adorned restaurants namely ‘The Harbour Court’ (multi cuisine), ‘Yue Chuan’ (Chinese) and ‘The Ocean’ (seafood) offers an array of international cuisine. It also has 4 bars with 2 outdoor venues, one indoor bar lounge and a trendy rooftop Sky Bar. All restaurants and banquet facilities are equipped with world- renowned Schonhuber Franchi & Sambonet cutlery, Spiegelau and Schott Zwiesel glassware and Villeroy & Boch crockery.

Location

Situated at the far end of the famous Galle Face Marine Drive and with the developing Colombo Port; travellers are presented with a fine view of the metropolitan life on one side from the hottest night spots, shopping malls and the best of Colombo’s cultural locations just minutes away, to the glistening Indian Ocean on the other side
