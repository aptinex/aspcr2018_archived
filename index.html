“Colours of Health and Disease”

Organized by

and
The Sri Lanka College of Dermatologists
15 th - 18 th August 2018
The Kingsbury, Colombo, Sri Lanka

Welcome to ASPCR - SLCD 2018

9th Meeting of Asian Society for Pigment Cell Research (ASPCR) organized jointly with Sri Lanka College of Dermatologists (SLCD) will be held on 15th-18th August  2018 at "The Kingsbury", Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Eminent speakers from all over the world will be gracing this event. In keeping with the theme, we look forward to sharing the “Colours of Health and Disease” with you.
